FAQ

FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) helps users find answers to common inquiries. It's a self-service tool designed to provide quick solutions and enhance user experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What gaming consoles do you offer?

We offer a wide range of gaming consoles including PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch. You can find the latest models and exclusive bundles on our website.

2. Do you sell gaming accessories?

Yes, we offer a variety of gaming accessories like controllers, headsets, gaming mice, and keyboard setups. We have options from top brands like Razer, Logitech, and SteelSeries.

3. Can I return a product if I am not satisfied?

Yes, we have a 14-day return policy on all our products. You can return any unused and unopened items within 14 days of receiving them for a full refund.

4. How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section. A tracking link will be provided for shipments once your order is dispatched.
